Back up a project automatically (autosave)

Camtasia automatically saves a backup of the Camtasia project (.cmproj) for recovery. The default interval between saves is one minute.

Change the autosave interval:

  1. Select Camtasia 2 > Preferences or press Command-comma.
  2. On the General tab, enter an interval in minutes to autosave the project.
