Mask or blur sensitive video content

Use the blur annotation to mask or blur portions of a video that contain sensitive content.

Blur video content:

  1. Click the Annotations tab.
  2. Click to select the blur animation. The styles appear below.
  3. Drag the blur annotation onto the canvas or timeline. Make sure the blur annotation is on the track above the item to blur on the timeline.
  4. To move the blur annotation, click within the annotation and drag it to a new position on the canvas.
  5. To resize the blur annotation, click to select the annotation on the canvas. Drag one of the annotation handles in or out.
  6. To edit the blur properties, click the Properties button .
  7. To change the duration the blur appears in the video, click to select the annotation on the timeline. Drag either end in or out to adjust the duration.
