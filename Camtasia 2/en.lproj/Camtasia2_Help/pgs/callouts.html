Add a callout

Add a callout to draw viewers' attention to important action on the screen. The following are examples of callouts:

Add a callout:

  1. Click the Annotations tab.
  2. Click to select the callout annotation. The preset styles appear below.
  3. Drag the desired style onto the canvas or timeline.
  4. To edit the text, double-click within the callout on the canvas. Enter the desired text.

Position, rotate, or resize:

  • To move the callout, click within the callout and drag it to a new position on the canvas.
  • To rotate the callout, click to select the callout on the canvas. Drag the rotation control around the anchor point.
  • To change the tail's position, click the callout tail and drag its anchor point around the callout on the canvas.
  • To resize the callout, click to select the callout on the canvas. Drag one of the annotation handles in or out.

Callout properties:

In the properties, you can change the text, color, style, outline, and opacity of a callout.

  • To view the annotation properties, click the Properties button . Click the Annotation Properties tab.
  • To view the text properties, click the Properties button . Click the Text Properties tab.
  • To reset to the style's default properties, click the Reset or Reset All button.
