Crop a clip

Crop mode allows you to select a portion of a full-screen recording or video clip. Use Crop mode to hide unwanted portions of a video or display a region of focus such as an application or website. The Media tab maintains the original source clip. Crop mode can also be used with callouts.

Crop:

  1. Click to select the video clip or callout to crop on the timeline.
  2. Click the Crop button . Blue handles appear around the selected media on the canvas.
  3. Drag the the handles in to crop out the unwanted portions of the video clip or callout.
  4. Click the Crop button to exit Crop mode.

    The canvas background appears in the final video for areas where there is not any media or video clips. You may want to resize the clip to fill the entire canvas on the canvas.
