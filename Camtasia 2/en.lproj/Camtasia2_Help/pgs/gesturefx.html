Gesture FX

Gesture effects simulate touchscreen interactions â€“ such as a double tap, swipe, or pinch. Gesture effects can be added to recording files (.trec or .cmrec), video clips, images, and most annotations.

Add a gesture effect:

  1. Click the Gesture FX tab.
  2. Drag the desired gesture effect onto an asset on the timeline.

Gesture effect properties:

In the properties, you can change the color, opacity, size, and easing of a gesture effect.

To edit the gesture effect properties:

  1. Select the asset on the timeline with the gesture effect.
  2. Click the Properties button to open the properties panel.
  3. Adjust the properties of the effect.

To revert to the default properties, click the Reset or Reset All button.
