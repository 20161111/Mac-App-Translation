Highlight within a video

Use the highlighter tool in the Annotations tab to highlight areas within a video.

Highlight an area:

  1. Click the Annotations tab.
  2. Click to view the highlight annotations. The styles appear below.
  3. Drag the highlight annotation onto the canvas or timeline. Make sure the highlight annotation is on the track above the item to highlight on the timeline.
  4. To move the highlight annotation, click within the annotation and drag it to a new position on the canvas.
  5. To resize the highlight annotation, click to select the annotation on the canvas. Drag one of the annotation handles in or out.
  6. To edit the highlight properties, click the Properties button.
  7. To change the duration the highlight appears in the video, click to select the annotation on the timeline. Drag the end in or out to adjust the duration.
