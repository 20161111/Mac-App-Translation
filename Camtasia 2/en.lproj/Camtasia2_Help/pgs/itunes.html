Share to iTunes

The Share to iTunes option allows you export your video with the appropriate dimensions and file format based on the device (iPhone 3GS, iPhone 4, iPad, or AppleTV) you plan to share to.

Share to iTunes:

  1. Select Share > iTunes.
  2. Enter filename and location.
  3. Select the device for playback.
  4. Click Export.

    A progress dialog appears and Camtasia begins exporting your video.
