Convert audio from stereo to mono

Stereo sound systems have two independent audio channels. If a single microphone input is used for recording, audio only plays in one speaker. Mix to Mono enables playback in both.

Enable Mix to Mono:

  1. Select an audio clip on the timeline.
  2. Click the Properties button .
  3. Click the Audio Properties tab.
  4. Check Mix to Mono.
