About Mobile Sharing with TechSmith Fuse

Mobile Sharing allows Camtasia to receive files from TechSmith Fuse. It is turned off by default and will only turn on if you do one of the following:

  • Connect TechSmith Fuse to Camtasia from a mobile device. See Import media from a mobile device.
  • Select Camtasia 2 > Preferences, then click Turn On Sharing.

Once Mobile Sharing is turned on, you can connect Fuse to Camtasia. Mobile Sharing will continue to run until it is turned off.

Turn off Mobile Sharing:

  1. Select Camtasia 2 > Preferences > General tab
  2. Click Turn Off Sharing.

Mobile Sharing may also turn off if interrupted. This can happen if:

  • The network/router becomes disconnected or needs to be reset.
  • Your computer automatically generates new IP addresses.
  • You change network providers.
