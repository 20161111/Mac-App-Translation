Properties

The properties panel allows you to adjust the settings for a selection on the timeline.

Copy and paste properties:

  1. Position the playhead over the frame whose properties you want to copy. Make sure that media clip is selected on the timeline
  2. Control-click on the selected media and choose Copy Properties.
  3. Reposition the playhead to where you want to paste the copied properties. Select that media clip.
  4. Control-click again on the selected media and choose Paste Properties.

To open the properties panel:

  • Click the Properties button .
  • Click View > Show Properties.
  • Press Command-2.

Properties tabs:

Each tab offers settings for media, annotation, and effects on the timeline and canvas. You must make a selection to view and adjust the properties.

  • Basic Properties & Effects: Fine tune the scale, opacity, rotation, and position for video clips. See Working with media on the canvas.
  • Audio Properties: Adjust the audio properties or settings for applied audio effects.
  • Text Properties: Adjust the callout and text properties such as font, color, style, alignment, etc.
  • Annotation Properties: Adjust the properties for annotations such as callouts, arrows, shapes, highlighter, and blur.
  • Cursor Properties: Adjust the properties for cursor effects.

Other options:

  • Reset: Click the Reset button to restore the original state of the selected media.
  • Delete: Click the Remove button on the left corner to delete a video, audio, or cursor effect.
  • Save annotation: Click the Save button to save the customized annotation to the Annotation tab.
