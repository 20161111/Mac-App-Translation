Recording file formats (.trec or .cmrec)

Recording files (.trec or .cmrec) contain the action recorded on the screen, as well as any audio and camera video recorded by Camtasia.

TechSmith recording files (.trec):

  • Screen recording files recorded with Camtasia Studio version 8.4.0 or later, or Camtasia for Mac version 2.8.0 or later. The recording files may also include audio and camera video.
  • TechSmith recording files are cross-compatible and can be used in Camtasia Studio projects (version 8.4.0 or later), or Camtasia for Mac projects (version 2.8.0 or later).
  • TechSmith recording files are automatically saved to Users > Movies > Camtasia 2 > Temporary Recordings folder.
  • You can apply cursor, SmartFocus, gesture, and video effects to TechSmith recording files.

Camtasia recording files (.cmrec):

  • Screen recording files recorded with Camtasia for Mac version 2.7.0 or earlier. The recording files may also include audio and camera video.
  • Camtasia recording files (.cmrec) are only compatible with Camtasia for Mac.
  • Camtasia recording files are automatically saved to Users > Movies > Camtasia 2 > Temporary Recordings folder.
  • You can apply cursor, SmartFocus, gesture, and video effects to Camtasia recording files.

Change the default save location:

By default, Camtasia saves the recording files to User > Movies > Camtasia 2 > Temporary Recordings foler for 14 days.

  1. In Camtasia, select Camtasia 2 > Preferences or press Command-comma.
  2. On the Recording tab, click the Change button.
  3. Choose where you want Camtasia to save your recording files, and then click the Select button.

Change the default time for saving recordings:

  1. In Camtasia, select Camtasia 2 > Preferences or press Command-comma.
  2. On the Recording tab, select the amount of time:
    • To specify the number of days, enter a number in the Days field.
    • To save recordings for an indefinite amount of time, disable the "Delete after" option.
