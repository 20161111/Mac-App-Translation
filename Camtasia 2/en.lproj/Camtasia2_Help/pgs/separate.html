Separate the video from the audio

The audio and video in recording files (.trec or .cmrec) are sometimes packaged as one clip. You can separate the screen video or camera video from the audio into two separate tracks for editing.

Separate the video and the audio:

  1. Control-click on the clip and select Separate Video and Audio.
  2. The audio and video separates into two clips on the timeline.

    Note: Moving the audio and video tracks on the timeline may cause the audio and video to become out of sync.
