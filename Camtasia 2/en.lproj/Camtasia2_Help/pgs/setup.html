Set up a recording

Camtasia records all activity on the screen within the selected recording area.

Set up a recording:

  1. Open Camtasia or choose File > New Recording.
  2. Select the screen area to record:
    • Full screen (default)
    • Another monitor
    • Mobile device
    • Region (preset, custom, or recently recorded region)
    • None (disable the Screen button to record audio or camera only)

See Select an area to record for more information on how to select the appropriate area based on what you are recording and how you plan to share your recording.

  1. Select the audio and camera options:
    • Camera video:
      Click to enable camera video. From the Camera menu, select the camera device, such as the built-in camera. A preview of the camera appears.



      Note: Camera video is automatically disabled for mobile recordings.
    • Microphone audio:
      Microphone audio is recorded by default. From the Audio menu, select the microphone.

    • System audio:
      Click to enable system audio recording. System audio is the sound from your device including mouse-click sounds, audio from your speakers, etc.



      If the TechSmith Audio Capture Component is not installed, click the Need System Audio help button to install it.
  1. Click the Record button or press Command-Shift-2 to start recording.

  2. Press Command-Shift-2 to pause the recording or press Command-Option-2 to stop the recording.

Camtasia adds the recording file (.trec or .cmrec) into the Media tab in the Assets and onto the timeline.
