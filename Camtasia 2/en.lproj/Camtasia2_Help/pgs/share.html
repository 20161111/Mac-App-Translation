Share your video

Camtasia offers several options to share your video with your intended audience. For best results:

  • Record at the same aspect ratio or dimensions recommended for the sharing destination.
  • Set the canvas dimensions before editing to the dimensions you plan to export the video.

Share to Screencast.com:

Upload your video directly to Screencast.com. Screencast.com is a media hosting site that allows you to maintain privacy control on your video, create a URL to share with your audience, and enable commenting.

  • Share to Screencast.com

Share to iTunes:

Export your video with the appropriate dimensions and file format based on the device (iPhone 3GS, iPhone 4, iPad, or AppleTV) you plan to share to.

  • Share to iTunes

Share to YouTube:

Upload your video directly to YouTube. Camtasia automatically selects the appropriate dimensions and settings for YouTube output.

  • Share to YouTube

Share to Google Drive:

Upload your video directly to Google Drive for sharing and collaboration.

  • Share to Google Drive

Share to My Places:

Upload your video directly to a shortcut in My Places, such as OneDrive for Business.

  • Share to My Places

Share to TechSmith Relay:

Upload your video directly to TechSmith Relay. This option only appears if the TechSmith Relay Recorder is installed.

  • Share to TechSmith Relay

Export MP4:

Select Share > Export to produce an MP4 file format for quick sharing on a website, blog, or another desired destination. Choose to embed the video in HTML.

  • Share to website or blog (MP4)
  • Embed video in a web page

Advanced export:

Select Share > Advanced Export to select custom export settings such as file format, codecs, audio settings, etc. for your desired destination.

  • Advanced export
  • Export audio

Other sharing options:

  • Export a video with captions
  • Add a table of contents
  • Export a captions file
  • View and share previous uploads in Share History
