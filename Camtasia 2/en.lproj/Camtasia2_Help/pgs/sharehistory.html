View and share previous uploads in Share History

The Share History maintains a record of videos previously uploaded to Screencast.com, YouTube, Google Drive, and TechSmith Relay from Camtasia.

The Share History window provides a:

  • URL for Google Drive and TechSmith Relay uploads
  • URL, embed code, and social outputs for Screencast.com and YouTube uploads.

View Share History:

  1. Select Share > Show History.
  2. The Share History window appears. Videos uploaded to Screencast.com, YouTube, Google Drive, or TechSmith Relay appear in a list to the left.


No entries appear in the Share History window if you delete your Share History or if you have not previously shared to Screencast.com, YouTube, Google Drive, or TechSmith Relay.

Copy or visit the video URL:

  1. Select an entry from the Share History list on the left.
  2. Click the Copy button to copy the URL to your clipboard or click the Visit button to open the video in a new web browser.

Copy the embed code:

This option is only available for Screencast.com and YouTube Share History entries.

  1. Select an entry from the Share History list.
  2. Click the Copy button to copy the embed code to your clipboard.

Post to Facebook:

This option is only available for Screencast.com and YouTube Share History entries.

  1. Click the Facebook icon .
  2. The Facebook dialog appears.
  3. If a Facebook account is not associated with your system, click Add Account.
  4. Edit the privacy settings, message, or location.
  5. Click Post.

Post to Twitter:

This option is only available for Screencast.com and YouTube Share History entries.

  1. Click the Twitter icon .
  2. The Twitter dialog appears.
  3. If a Twitter account is not linked with Camtasia for Mac, click Add Account.
  4. Edit your message or location.
  5. Click Send.

Search Share History:

The title and share destination of a video are both searchable.

  1. Enter a search term into the Search field.
  2. To clear the Search field, click .

Delete Share History entries:

Control-click and select Remove Entry or Remove All Entries. You can also press the Delete key.

Note: Deleting entries from the Share History window permanently removes these items. You cannot recover a Share History entry if it has been deleted.
