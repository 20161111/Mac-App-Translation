Change the startup application

When you open Camtasia, the editing options appear by default. You can choose to show the recording options or the editing options on startup within the Camtasia Preferences dialog.

Change the startup application:

  1. Select Camtasia 2 > Preferences or press Command-comma.
  2. On the General tab, select Recorder or Editor from the On startup open option.
  3. Close the Preferences dialog.
