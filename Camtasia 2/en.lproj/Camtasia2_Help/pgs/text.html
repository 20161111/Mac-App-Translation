Add text

Add text to enhance your video.

Add text:

  1. Click the Annotations tab.
  2. Click to select the text callouts. The preset styles appear below.

  3. Drag the desired style onto the canvas or timeline.
  4. To edit the text, double-click within the callout on the canvas. Enter the desired text.

Position, rotate, or resize the text:

  • To move, click within the callout and drag it to a new position on the canvas.
  • To rotate, click to select the callout on the canvas. Drag the rotation control around the anchor point.
  • To resize, click to select the callout on the canvas. Drag one of the handles in or out.

Text properties:

In the properties, you can change the font, color, size, style, and alignment.

  • To view the text properties, click the Properties button . Click the Text Properties tab.
  • To reset to the style's default properties, click the Reset or Reset All button.
