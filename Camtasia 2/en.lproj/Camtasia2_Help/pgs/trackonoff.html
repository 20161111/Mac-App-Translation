Turn track content off or on

Turn a track off to prevent auditory and visual content from appearing in the canvas, preview, or rendered video. A media track is turned on by default.

Turn a track off:

Click the eye icon to the left of the track. The icon turns blue and the track dims to indicate the track is off.


Turn a track on:

Click the eye icon to the left of the track. The icon turns gray and the track brightens to indicate the track is on.

You can also turn tracks on or off using the context menus. Control-click the track and select Turn Track On or Turn Track Off.
