Troubleshooting mobile recording

If you are having difficulty recording a mobile device, see the following topics for more information:

  • Device not listed or no signal detected
  • Image Capture launches before or after recording
  • Recording does not pick up system audio

Device not listed or no signal detected:

If your device is not listed in the Recorder dropdown, or no signal is detected, make sure:

  • Your mobile device is running iOS 8 and your computer is running Mac OS X Yosemite.
  • The Lightning cable is fully connected to both your mobile device and computer.
  • Your mobile device is on and active.

Image Capture launches before or after recording

Depending on your system settings, an import tool called Image Capture may launch when you connect your device to your computer.

To disable this behavior:

  1. Connect your iOS device.
  2. If Image Capture does not launch automatically, open it from your Applications.
  3. Select your device from the list of devices.
  4. Click the button in the lower left corner to expand the device settings.
  5. Select No application from the dropdown.

Recording does not pick up system audio

If system audio is enabled in the Recorder but does not end up in your final recording, make sure:

  • Your mobile device has the sound or ringer turned on.
  • The sound settings for the application are turned on.
