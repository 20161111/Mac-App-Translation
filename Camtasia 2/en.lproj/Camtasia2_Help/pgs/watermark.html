Watermark a video

Learn how to add an image throughout the duration of the video as a watermark. Add a watermark image to:

  • Identify the video owner/creator.
  • Protect intellectual property.
  • Brand a video with a company's or institution's logo.

Watermark a video:

  1. Select File > Import Media.
  2. Select an image to use as the watermark.
  3. Click the Media tab.
  4. Drag the image onto a blank track on the timeline above the current clip.
  5. To position the image, drag the image on the canvas.
  6. Click the Properties button to edit settings such as Opacity.
  7. To display the watermark through the entire video, drag the right edge of the watermark image to the end of the timeline.
