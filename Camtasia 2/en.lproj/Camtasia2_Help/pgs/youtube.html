Share to YouTube

Upload your video directly to YouTube. Camtasia automatically selects the appropriate settings for YouTube output. For best results, adjust the canvas size before editing to one of YouTube's output dimensions: 640x360, 1280x720 (720p HD), or 1920x1080 (1080p HD).

Share to YouTube:

  1. Select Share > YouTube.
  2. The Google Account dialog appears. Enter your email and password, then click Sign In. If you do not have a Google account, you will need to create one to continue.
  3. Read through the terms of service and click Accept.
  4. Enter the title, tags, and description for your video. Tags should be single-word descriptors of your video, separated by commas.
  5. Set the privacy level.
    • If you have markers in your video and want to generate a table of contents from those, check that option. See Add a table of contents. The table of contents will display in the video description field on YouTube.
  1. Click Share. An export dialog appears. The Share History window opens when the export is complete and finishes the upload process. Use the URL, embed code, or social outputs to share your video.
