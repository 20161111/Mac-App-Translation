Add media or assets to a video

Add media, annotations, transitions, animations, and effects to the timeline to create a video.

Add a recording or imported media to a video:

  1. Click the Media tab.
  2. Click and drag the desired media onto the timeline, or Control-click on the media and select Add to Timeline at Playhead. Shift-click or Command-click to select multiple pieces of media.

Add other assets or effects to a video:

  1. Click the Annotations, Transitions, Animations, Video FX, Audio FX, Cursor FX, or Gesture FX tab.
  2. Drag the desired asset or effect onto the timeline or canvas.
  3. To learn how to edit properties, see:
    • Add a callout
    • Add text
    • Add an arrow or line
    • Add a shape
    • Blur sensitive video content
    • Transitions
    • Animations
    • Video FX
    • Cursor FX
    • Gesture FX
