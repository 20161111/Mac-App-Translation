Animations

Enhance your video by adding animations to a video clip or annotation on the timeline.

Add an animation

  1. Click the Animations tab.
  2. Drag the desired animation onto a video clip or annotation on the timeline. Position the left side of the arrow where you want the animation to start.

Customize the animation properties:

  1. Click the tip of the arrowhead.
  2. Click the Properties button to open the properties panel. Click the Basic Properties & Effects tab to adjust the animation's final effect.

Change the duration:

The default duration for animations is one second. To change this setting, see Change the default duration.

  1. Click to select the animation on the timeline.
  2. Drag one of the ends of the animation in or out. Adjusting the duration changes where the animation occurs within the media or annotation.

Copy and paste animations:

  1. Click to select the animation on the timeline.
  2. Use the hotkey Command-C to copy the animation.
  3. Reposition the playhead to where you want the pasted animation to end.
  4. Use the hotkey Command-V to paste the animation on the clip.

Turn easing on or off:

  1. Click an animation on the timeline to select it.

  2. Control-click the animation and choose Exponential In/Out or None from the menu.
