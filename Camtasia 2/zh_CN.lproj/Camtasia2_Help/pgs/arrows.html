Add an arrow or line

Add an arrow or line annotation to enhance your videos:

  • Add an arrow to draw viewers' attention to important action on the screen.
  • Add an animated line or arrow to illustrate progression over time.
  • Add a line to visually connect media within a video.

Add an arrow or line:

  1. Click the Annotations tab.
  2. Click to select the arrow or line annotation. The preset styles appear below.


  3. Drag the desired style onto the canvas or timeline.

Move, rotate, or resize:

  • To move the annotation, click within the annotation and drag it to a new position on the canvas.
  • To rotate the annotation, click to select the annotation on the canvas. Drag the rotation control around the anchor point.
  • To resize the annotation, click to select the annotation on the canvas. Drag one of the annotation handles in or out.

Arrow or line properties:

In the properties, you can change the color, style, width, and opacity of an arrow or line.

  • To view the arrow or line properties, click the Properties button . Click the Annotation Properties tab.
  • To reset to the style's default properties, click the Reset or Reset All button.
