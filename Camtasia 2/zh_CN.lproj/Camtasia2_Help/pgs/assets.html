Assets and effects

Assets are recordings, imported media, annotations, transitions, animations, and effects available in a project that can be used to create a video. To show or hide the available assets, click the Assets and Effects button.

Media:

The Media tab contains recording files (.trec or .cmrec) and imported media (video clips, audio clips, or still images) within a project. Use these source files to build your video.

See Import media and Add media or assets to a video

Annotations:

The Annotations tab contains the text, callout, arrow, shape, highlighter, and blur tools to enhance your video.

  • Add a callout
  • Add a sketch motion callout
  • Add a keystroke callout
  • Add an arrow or line
  • Add a shape
  • Highlight within a video
  • Blur a portion of the video

Transitions:

Transitions add a visual effect to the beginning or end of a piece of media or annotation on the timeline.

See Transitions

Animations:

Animations apply to media or annotations over an interval of time.

See Animations

Video effects:

Video effects add a visual filter over the entire duration or a portion of a clip, image, or annotation on the timeline.

See Video FX

Audio effects:

Audio effects add an audio filter over the entire duration or a portion of an audio clip.

Cursor effects:

Cursor effects focus viewers' attention on cursor movement and mouse clicks.

See Cursor FX
