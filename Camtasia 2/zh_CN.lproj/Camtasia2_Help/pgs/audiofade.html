Fade the audio in or out

Transitions add an effect to the beginning or end of a piece of media on the timeline.

Fade audio:

  1. Click the Transitions tab.
  2. Drag the Audio Fade In/Out transition onto the beginning or end of an audio clip or annotation on the timeline.
  3. Click to select the transition on the timeline. To adjust the duration of the audio fade, drag the edge of the transition in or out.
  4. To customize the audio fade properties, click the Properties button .
