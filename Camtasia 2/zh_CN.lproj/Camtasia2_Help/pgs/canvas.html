Canvas

The canvas displays a preview of the media within the video at the location of the playhead on the timeline.

You can position and edit the assets within a video directly on the canvas. See Working with media on the canvas. Any portion of the media or assets located outside the highlighted canvas area will not appear in the final video.

To preview how the final video will appear exported at the current canvas dimensions, set the canvas zoom level to 100% (actual size).

Canvas keyboard shortcuts:

  • Zoom In: Command-=
  • Zoom Out: Command--(minus)
  • Zoom to Fit: Command-0
  • Zoom to 100%: Option-Z
