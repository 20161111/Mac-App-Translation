Change the canvas editing dimensions

The default canvas background color is black. If the media and assets in the video do not cover the entire canvas area, the background appears in the final video.

Change the canvas dimensions:

  1. Control-click on the canvas and select Adjust Canvas or select Adjust Canvas from the canvas preview dropdown.
  2. The canvas controls appear. Camtasia dims the entire application except for the canvas area.
  3. Enter the new dimensions in the width and height fields (check the box to the right to maintain aspect ratio) or select a preset from the dropdown.
  4. Click Apply.
