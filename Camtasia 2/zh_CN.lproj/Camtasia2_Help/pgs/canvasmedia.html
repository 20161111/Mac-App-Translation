Working with media on the canvas

Note: Any portion of the media located outside the highlighted canvas area is not included in the final video.

Position media:

  1. Click to select the media on the canvas.
  2. Drag the media to the desired position on the canvas.

  3. As you drag media on the canvas, yellow lines appear to help align or center the media. When the media is centered on the canvas, yellow lines appear through the center point of the media. Press Command-; to hide or show the guides.

Resize media:

  1. Click to select the media on the canvas.
  2. Drag a handle on the selected media on the canvas.

For precise editing, use the Scale control located within the properties panel.

Rotate media:

  1. Click to select the media on the canvas.
  2. Drag the rotation control on the canvas around the anchor point.
