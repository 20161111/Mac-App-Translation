Canvas view settings

The canvas displays a preview of the video at the playhead's location on the timeline. Use the following canvas view options when editing your video:

Change the canvas preview scale:

For precise editing, change the canvas zoom level to zoom in or out. Changing this scale does not affect the final appearance of the video, only the current view.

To preview how the final video will look at the current canvas dimensions, set the canvas zoom level to 100%.

  • Click the Canvas Zoom Level dropdown menu and select a percentage.
  • Use two fingers to scroll up or down on the trackpad.

Move or pan the canvas:

To view a hidden area when zoomed in on the canvas, hold the spacebar and drag the video area on the canvas.
