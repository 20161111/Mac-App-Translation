Change the caption duration

By default, captions are divided into four-second segments and span the entire duration of the audio clip.

Change the caption duration:

The caption duration must be between two and seven seconds to be ADA compliant.

  1. Click to select a caption segment. The caption editor appears.
  2. Drag the Duration slider in the caption editor. The duration (in seconds) for the selected caption segment appears under the slider.

Split captions:

Splitting captions divides the caption into two equal parts. The existing caption text is repeated in both segments.

  1. Click to select a caption segment. The caption editor appears.
  2. Control-click the caption segment and select Split Caption or click the Caption Options dropdown in the caption editor and select Split Current Caption.

Merge captions:

Merging captions combines two captions into one. The existing caption text is also combined.

  1. Click to select a caption segment. The caption editor appears.
  2. Control-click the caption segment and select Merge with next Caption or click the Caption Options dropdown in the caption editor and select Merge with next Caption.
