Camtasia Studio vs. Camtasia for Mac

With their own distinct platform personalities, Camtasia Studio and Camtasia for Mac do not share all the same features and functionality. While there is some overlap, each product offers unique features in order to solve problems specific to Windows or Mac users. For this reason, Camtasia Studio and Camtasia for Mac are priced differently and require separate licenses. Check out the comparison chart below to determine which product best fits your needs.
