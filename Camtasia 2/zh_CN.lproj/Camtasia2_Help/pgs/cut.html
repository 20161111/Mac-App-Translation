Cut, copy, or paste

Cut, copy, or paste media, assets, or a selection.

Cut:

  1. Make a selection on the timeline.
  2. Select Edit > Cut or press Command-X.

Ripple cut:

Ripple cut removes the blank space between the cut media on the timeline. The Media tab preserves the original source file.

  1. Make a selection on the timeline.
  2. Select Edit > Ripple Cut or press Command-Shift-X.

Stitched media:

When you remove a section from a clip, join the remaining clips together to easily animate across them.

  1. Adjust split clips so that they are adjoined on the timeline.
  2. Control-click and select Stitch Media or press Command-Option-I.
  3. To unstitch media, Control-click on a stitched media clip and choose Unstitch Media.

Copy:

  1. Make a selection on the timeline.
  2. Select Edit > Copy or press Command-C.

Paste:

  1. Position the playhead on the timeline where you want to insert the selection.
  2. If you want to insert the selection within a clip, select Edit > Split Selected at Playhead or Split All at Playhead. You may need to move clips on the timeline to make space to paste the selection.
  3. Select Edit > Paste or press Command-V.
