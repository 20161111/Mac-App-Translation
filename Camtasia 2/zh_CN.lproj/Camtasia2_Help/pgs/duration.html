Change the duration of assets on the timeline

You can change the duration for individual pieces of media and assets on the timeline. The default duration for images, shapes, and text is five seconds. The default duration for transitions and animations is one second.

Change the duration:

  1. Click to select the asset on the timeline.
  2. Drag the end point to increase or decrease the duration of the asset on the timeline.
