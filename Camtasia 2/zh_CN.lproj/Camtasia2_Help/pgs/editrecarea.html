Edit the recording area

After selecting the recording area, you can move or resize the area before recording. Once you begin recording, you cannot edit this area.

Move the recording area:

Click within the recording area and drag it to another location on the screen.

Resize the recording area:

  • Manually: Click and drag on the green border of the recording area or enter new dimensions into the dimension fields.


  • Region menu: Select a preset or recently recorded area from the dropdown menu.

Tip for setting up the recording area:

You cannot access any applications or system settings while in the selection mode. Make sure to edit the audio settings, application or window position, and other system settings before selecting the area to record. Click the Cancel Selection button or press Esc to edit these items.
