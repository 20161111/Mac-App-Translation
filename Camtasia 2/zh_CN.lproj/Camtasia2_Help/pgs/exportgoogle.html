Share to Google Drive

Upload your video directly to Google Drive for sharing and collaboration.

Upload a video to Google Drive:

  1. Select Share > Google Drive.
  2. The Google Account dialog appears. Enter your email and password, then click Sign in. If you do not have a Google account, you will need to create one to continue.
  3. Read through the terms of service and click Accept.
  4. Enter a title and select a folder to upload your video to. The default is a TechSmith folder created by Camtasia.
  5. Click Share. An export dialog appears. The Share History window opens when the export is complete and finishes the upload process. Use the URL or social outputs to share your video with others.
