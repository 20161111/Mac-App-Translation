Fit to visible area on canvas

The Fit to Visible canvas option adjusts the canvas size to the visible content on the canvas.

Fit to visible area:

  1. For best results, click and drag the clip(s) on the canvas to center your content within the canvas.
  2. Select Edit > Adjust Canvas.
  3. The Canvas controls appear. Camtasia dims the entire application except for the canvas area. Select the Fit to Visible option from the dropdown menu.
  4. Click Apply.
