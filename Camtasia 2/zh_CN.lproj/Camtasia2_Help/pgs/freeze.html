Using Freeze Region

Freeze regions of your video clips to hide mistakes or pop-ups in your original recordings.

Apply Freeze Region to your video:

  1. Click and drag the Freeze Region video effect from the Video FX tab onto media that has been added to the timeline. The video effect will appear in the FX drawer.
  2. On the canvas, adjust the region borders over the area to freeze.



  3. In the FX drawer, select and drag the edges of the effect to adjust its duration. The left edge of the Freeze Region effect determines when the region of the frame freezes.

  4. To adjust the effect's properties, click on the Properties button to open the properties panel.
  5. Choose the shape of the region, blend or harden the border, change the transparency, and round or square the edges in the properties panel. Preview the effect on the canvas.
