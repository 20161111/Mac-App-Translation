Hotspots

Add an interactive hotspot to direct viewers with a hyperlink, take viewers to specific markers in your video, or allow viewers to replay or jump to a section of the video.

Add a hotspot:

  1. Click and drag the Interactive Hotspot effect from the Video FX tab onto the timeline or canvas to the desired clip, callout, or text annotation.
  2. Open the properties panel and edit the Interactive Hotspot section. See Hotspot properties.
  3. To share your interactive video, follow the instructions for embedding a video on a web page or blog, or share to Screencast.com. These are the only sharing options recommended, since the TechSmith Smart Player is required for the hotspots to function.

Hotspot properties:

Pause at end: Viewer must click to continue viewing the video.

URL: Opens a web page when clicked. Enter the web address and press tab or return on your keyboard.

Marker: The video jumps to a marker in the video. Select a marker from the dropdown.

Time: The video jumps to a specific frame. Enter the frame to jump to in Minutes:Seconds:Frames.

Click to continue: The viewer must click to continue viewing the video.
