Keystroke callouts

Use keystroke callouts to show keyboard activity in your video. For example, if you press Command-A on the keyboard as you are recording, use the keystroke callouts to show that keyboard combination in your video.

Add a keystroke callout:

  1. Click the Annotations tab.
  2. Click to select the Keystroke callouts. The preset styles appear below.

  3. Drag the desired style onto the canvas or timeline.
  4. To edit the callout, select it and then open the properties by clicking on the Properties button .

Keystroke properties:

In the properties, you can change the style and key combination.

  • To change the style or key combination, open the properties. Click the Annotation Properties tab.
  • To reset to the style's default properties, click the Reset or Reset All button.
