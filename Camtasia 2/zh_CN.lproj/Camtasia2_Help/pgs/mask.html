Mask a clip

Use the mask effect to selectively hide or reveal parts of a clip. For example:

  • Show selective parts of a media clip through an oval spotlight shape
  • Partially reveal video or image clips playing through a title or logo graphic

Add a mask:

  1. Click and drag the Mask effect from the Video FX tab onto media that has been added to the timeline or canvas. This is how your timeline should look:


    By default, that media will be masked except for a small oval, showing mostly the content on the track below.



  2. Click on the Properties button to open the properties panel.
  3. Select the properties and preview the effect on the canvas.
