Preview the recording

After recording, the recording file (.trec or .cmrec) appears in the Media tab.

Preview in the Media tab:

Click the clip to preview on the Media tab. The video preview loops.

Preview on the canvas:

  1. Drag the selected clip from the Media tab onto the timeline.
  2. Move the playhead to the beginning of the clip.
  3. To view the clip in its actual dimensions, make sure the canvas scale is set to 100%.

  4. Click the Play button or press the spacebar to start playback.
