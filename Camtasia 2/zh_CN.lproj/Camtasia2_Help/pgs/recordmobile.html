Record a mobile device

Camtasia records all activity on your screen within the selected recording area. Record your mobile device to create app previews, demonstrations, or other mobile-specific content.

Note: Mobile recording only works with iOS 8 devices. The device must be plugged in via Lightning cable to a computer running OS X Yosemite v10.10.

Record a mobile device:

  1. Connect your mobile device to your computer via Lightning cable. You need to have iOS 8 installed on your mobile device and OS X Yosemite on your computer.
  2. Open Camtasia and choose File > New Recording.
  3. Select your mobile device from the Recorder dropdown.
  4. Select the audio options:
  • Microphone audio:
    Microphone audio is recorded by default. From the Audio menu, select the microphone.
  • System audio:
    Click to enable system audio recording. System audio is the sound from your mobile device, including application music, notification sounds, etc.


    If the TechSmith Audio Capture Component is not installed, click the Need System Audio help button to install it.
  1. Click the Record button or press Command-Shift-2 to start recording.
  2. Press Command-Shift-2 to pause the recording or Command-Option-2 to stop the recording.

Camtasia adds the recording file (.mov) into the Media tab in the Assets and onto the timeline.
