Ripple delete

You can delete an entire clip or a portion of a clip on the timeline. Ripple delete removes the extra space between the media on the timeline. When deleting clips, the media bin preserves the original source file.

Ripple delete:

  1. Select the media to delete:
    • Single clip: Click a clip.
    • Multiple clips: Hold the Shift or Command key and click the clips.
    • Portion of a clip: Hold the Shift key and drag an area on the timeline.
  1. Control-click and select Ripple Delete, Delete Range, or from the menu select Edit > Ripple Delete.

    Ripple delete removes the extra space between media when deleting.
