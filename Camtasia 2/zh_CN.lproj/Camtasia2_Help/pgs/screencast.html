Share to Screencast.com

Upload your video directly to Screencast.com. Screencast.com provides a URL and an embed code that allows you to share the content with your audience. Screencast.com never compresses or alters your content, so what you upload is what viewers see. The four privacy levels allow you to control who can view your media. For more information, visit Screencast.com.

Share to Screencast.com:

  1. Select Share > Screencast.com
  2. The Screencast.com dialog appears. Enter your email and password, then click Sign In. If you are a new user, click the Create New Account button.
  1. Enter a title.
  2. Adjust other options as needed.
    • Folder: Choose which Screencast.com folder you want to send your video to.
    • Table of contents: Select the Create table of contents from markers option if your timeline contains markers and you want to add a table of contents to your video.
    • Captions style: Choose from Closed or Under video captions if your video contains captions.
  1. Click Share. An export dialog appears. The Share History window opens when the export is complete and finishes the upload process. Use the URL, embed code, or social outputs to share your video.
