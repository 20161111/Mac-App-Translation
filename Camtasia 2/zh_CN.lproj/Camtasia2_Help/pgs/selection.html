Make a selection on the timeline

To edit an object, media, or portion of the video, you must first make a selection on the timeline. Also, learn how to make a selection to insert time and ripple shift a selection.

Select media (clip or annotation):

  • Click on the desired media on the timeline.
  • To select multiple clips or annotations, Shift-click or Command-click on the desired media on the timeline.

Camtasia highlights the selected media in blue.

Select a portion across a single track:

Hold the Shift key and drag your cursor along the desired track.

Camtasia highlights the selected area.

Select a portion across multiple tracks:

  • Hold the Shift key and drag your cursor over the desired area on the timeline.
  • Hold the Shift key and drag the playhead.

Camtasia highlights the selected area.

Insert time:

  1. Hold the Shift key and drag your cursor over the desired area to make a selection on the timeline.
  2. Control-click on the selection and choose Insert Time.

Undo a selection:

  • Click within an open track to clear the current selection.
  • Shift-click or Command-click to remove individual media or annotations from the selection.
