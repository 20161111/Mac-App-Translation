Add a shape

Add a shape annotation to enhance your video:

  • Add a shape to draw viewers' attention to important action on the screen.
  • Add a shape to mask or block out sensitive items from appearing within a video.

Add a shape:

  1. Click the Annotations tab.
  2. Click to select the shape annotation. The preset styles appear below.

  3. Drag the desired style onto the canvas or timeline.

Position, rotate, or resize the annotation:

  • To move, click within the annotation and drag it to a new position on the canvas.
  • To rotate, click to select the annotation on the canvas. Drag the rotation control around the anchor point.
  • To resize, click to select the annotation on the canvas. Drag one of the annotation handles in or out.

Shape properties:

In the properties, you can change the color, style, width, and opacity of a shape.

  • To view the shape properties, click the Properties button . Click the Annotation Properties tab.
  • To reset to the style's default properties, click the Reset or Reset All button.
