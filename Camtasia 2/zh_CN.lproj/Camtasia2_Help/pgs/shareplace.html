Share to My Places

Upload your video to OneDrive for Business to access it at any time across computers or devices.

Share to My Places

  1. Select Share > My Places > OneDrive for Business.

    If you have not previously shared to My Places, see Add or remove a Place.
  2. Enter a filename.
  3. Drag the slider to adjust the video quality and file size.
  4. Click the Browse button to select a location for the video.
  5. Click Share.

A MP4 video file is uploaded to the selected My Places location. The URL is copied onto the clipboard.
