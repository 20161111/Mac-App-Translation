Add a sketch motion callout

Sketch motion callouts are drawn on the screen over a period of time. Customizations include drawing time, color, fade length, and more.

Add a sketch motion callout:

  1. Click the Annotations tab.
  2. Click to select the sketch motion callouts. The preset styles appear below.

  3. Drag the desired style onto the canvas or timeline.
  4. To edit the color, size, and other properties of the callout, select it on the canvas or timeline and then open the properties by clicking the Properties button .

Sketch motion properties:

In the properties, you can change the color, width, size, opacity, and draw time of a sketch motion callout.

  • To change the scale, opacity, angle, or position: click the Properties button and then the Basic Properties and Effects tab.
  • To change the color, width, or draw time: click the Properties button and then the Annotations Properties tab.
  • To reset to the style's default properties, click the Reset or Reset All button.
