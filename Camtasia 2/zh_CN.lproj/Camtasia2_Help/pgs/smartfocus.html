SmartFocus animation

SmartFocus automatically zooms in on areas in a recording file (.trec or .cmrec) based on the activity within the recording. When recording, Camtasia collects data about the actions completed and the cursor movement in the recording. SmartFocus predicts where to add zoom effects, saving you the time it takes to manually find and insert actions.

SmartFocus cannot be applied to region recordings and file formats other than recording files (.trec or .cmrec) recorded with Camtasia.

Add a SmartFocus animation:

  1. Click the Animation tab.
  2. Drag the SmartFocus animation onto a recording file (.trec or .cmrec).
  3. SmartFocus automatically generates zoom actions based on the activity within the recording file.
