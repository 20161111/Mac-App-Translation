Spotlight

Use light to draw attention to areas of the canvas. Choose from a variety of spotlight colors, and customize the direction, brightness, focus, and opacity.

Apply spotlight:

  1. Click and drag the Spotlight effect from the Video FX tab to a clip on the canvas or timeline. By default, the spotlight is tied to the upper left-hand corner of the media clip.

  2. Click and drag the light icon to adjust the light source, and the anchor point to adjust the direction.

Edit the effect:

  1. Click the Properties button to open the properties panel.
  2. Edit the spotlight properties in the Basic Properties and Effects tab.
