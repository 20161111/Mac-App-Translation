Timeline

Use the timeline to arrange the sequence for all media and assets within a video. The playhead position on the timeline determines the display of the video on the canvas. The timecode is shown to the right of the playhead in Hours:Minutes:Seconds;Frames.

Timeline tracks:

Tracks represent the sequence of all media within a video. Drag media and assets onto a track on the timeline to include it in the video. See Timeline tracks.

Timeline zoom:

Drag the Timeline zoom slider to zoom in or out on the timeline.

Click to zoom out or click to zoom in on the timeline.
