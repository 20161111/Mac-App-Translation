Transitions

Transitions add a visual effect to the beginning or end of a piece of media or annotation on the timeline.

Add a transition:

  1. Click the Transitions tab.
  2. Drag the desired transition onto the beginning or end of selected media on the timeline.

Change the duration:

By default, transitions apply to media for one second. To change the default duration, see Change the default duration.

  1. Click to select the transition on the timeline.
  2. Drag the end to extend the duration.

Remove a transition:

Click to select the transition on the timeline. Press the Delete key.
