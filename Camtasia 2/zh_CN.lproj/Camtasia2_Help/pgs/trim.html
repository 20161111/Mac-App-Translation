Trim media

You can trim out the beginning or end of media on the timeline. Trimming does not permanently delete any portion of the media.

Trim media:

  1. Click to select the media on the timeline.
  2. Drag the end of the media in to trim to media.

  3. Drag the end of the media back out to restore the media.

Ripple trim media:

  1. Shift-click to select multiple clips on the timeline.
  2. Drag the end of one media clip in to trim all selected media.
  3. Drag the end of the media back out to restore the media.
