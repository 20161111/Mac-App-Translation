Video FX

Video effects add a visual filter over the entire duration or a portion of a clip, image, or annotation on the timeline.

Video FX:

  1. Click the Video FX tab.
  2. Drag the desired video effects onto the media on the timeline.

Customize the video effects properties:

  1. Click to select the media on the timeline with the video effects.
  2. Click the Properties button to open the properties.
  3. Click the Basic Properties & Effects tab to adjust the video effects properties. See Properties.

Change the duration:

By default, video effects apply to the entire duration of a Camtasia recording on the timeline.

  1. Click the arrow on the bottom of the clip on the timeline to show the applied effects.
  2. Drag the ends of the video effects to adjust the duration.

Remove video effects:

  • From the timeline: Click the arrow on the bottom of the media on the timeline to show the applied effects. Click to select the effect to delete. Press the Delete key.
  • From the properties: Click to select the media on the timeline. On the Basic Properties & Effects tab, click the Remove button for the desired effect.
