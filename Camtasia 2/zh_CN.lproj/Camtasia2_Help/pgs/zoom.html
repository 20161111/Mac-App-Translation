Zooming in or out on your video

Enhance your video by showing context or details with zoom animations.

Add a zoom animation:

  1. Click the Animations tab.
  2. Drag the Zoom In or Zoom Out animation onto a video clip or annotation on the timeline. Position the left side of the arrow where you want to start zooming.

Customize the animation properties:

  1. Click the tip of the animation arrowhead on the timeline.
  2. Click the Properties button to open the properties panel. Click the Basic Properties & Effects tab and use the Scale slider control to adjust the animation's final effect.

Change the duration:

The default duration (and speed) for animations is one second. To change this setting, see Change the default duration.

  1. Click to select the animation on the timeline.
  2. Drag one of the ends of the animation in or out. Adjusting the duration changes where the animation occurs within the media or annotation.

Copy and paste zoom animations:

  1. Click to select the animation on the timeline.
  2. Use the hotkey Command-C to copy the animation.
  3. Reposition the playhead to where you want the pasted animation to end.
  4. Use the hotkey Command-V to paste the animation on the clip.
