Document Organizer

Document Organizer Preferences

The Document Organizer window has two preference settings that you can turn on to customize its behavior. The "Always show Document Organizer on launch" setting, when enabled, will display the Document Organizer window on every launch except when running a scheduled synchronization.

The "Auto-show/hide the Document Organizer" option, when enabled, will automatically show/hide as you open documents or switch applications.

Both of these settings can be found in the "Window Behavior" section within the General Preferences.
