Tips

ChronoSync takes full advantage of tool tips within the application. Simply roll your mouse over a feature, wait a moment, and a tool tip will appear to guide you.

Throughout ChronoSync's interface, you'll find "Help" links that, when clicked, will take you to a specific Help page to help you in that area of the application.

Get several handy guides for using ChronoSync on our website. These tips will help you broaden your knowledge of what ChronoSync can do for you.

If you cannot find the information you need in the online help or by browsing the tips, download the ChronoSync manual, located on the installer DMG.
