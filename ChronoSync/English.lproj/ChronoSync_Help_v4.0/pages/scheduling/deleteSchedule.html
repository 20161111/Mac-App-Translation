Scheduling

Delete a Schedule

The entries listed in the "Scheduled Documents Manager" may be deleted if, for whatever reason, you no longer need that particular document to be scheduled.

Deleting an entry in the Scheduled Documents Manager:

  1. To open the "Scheduled Documents Manager", choose "Scheduled Documents Manager" from the "Window" menu.
  2. Select/highlight the entry in the "Scheduled Documents Manager" that you would like to delete.
  3. To delete, click on the button on the window.
  4. A warning message will appear to ensure that you really wish to delete the document from scheduling. If you are certain, choose "OK".
  5. You will notice that the entry no longer appears in the list.

Note: If you would like to temporarily disable a scheduled document, but not permanently delete it, then you can just uncheck the checkbox next to the scheduled document in the "Scheduled Documents Manager" window. To reactivate it, you simply check the checkbox.
