Scheduling

Edit a Schedule

Once a synchronization has been scheduled, it can be changed by taking advantage of the edit function within the "Scheduled Documents Manager".

Editing an entry in the Scheduled Documents Manager:

  1. To open the "Scheduled Documents Manager", choose "Scheduled Documents Manager" from the "Window" menu.
  2. Select/highlight the entry in the "Scheduled Documents Manager" that you would like to edit.
  3. To edit, click on the button on the window.
  4. After the "Edit Scheduled Synchronization" window appears, change the scheduling settings to your liking.
  5. If you are satisfied with how the schedule is set up, then choose "OK".

Note: Double-clicking on the entry in the list will also open the "Edit Scheduled Synchronization" window.
