Analyze Panel Functions

The hierarchal view can be customized in various ways. You can rearrange the columns, choose which column to sort the entries, or size the columns.

To rearrange the columns:

  1. Click on the column’s label.
  2. Drag it to the desired location.
  3. Release the mouse.

To sort the columns:

  1. Click on the column’s label.
  2. A small arrow pointing up means that the column data is sorted in ascending order. Conversely, an arrow pointing down displays the sorted data in descending order. Clicking on the sorted column’s label will toggle the sort order between ascending and descending.

To size the columns:

  1. Click on the column’s label.
  2. Each column's label is separated by a vertical line. Point the mouse to the line.
  3. You will see the mouse pointer turn into arrows. Drag it to the desired size.
  4. Release the mouse.
