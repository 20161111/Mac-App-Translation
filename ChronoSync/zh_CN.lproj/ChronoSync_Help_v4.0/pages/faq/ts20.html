ChronoSync FAQ's

I followed the instructions for scheduling a daily backup but whenever I click on the "Add to Schedule..." button, the schedule dialogue box indicates that I am scheduled for a weekly backup. What gives?

The "Scheduled Documents Manager" window is a list of all your scheduled Synchronizer documents. Double click any scheduled entry to edit it. Clicking the "Add to Schedule..." button on the toolbar simply adds a NEW schedule time to the Synchronizer document. It does not change existing scheduled times.
