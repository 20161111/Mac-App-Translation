ChronoSync FAQ's

How do I run a scheduled sync directly from the ChronoSync system menu?

If you need a scheduled sync to run sooner than its scheduled time, an easy way to do this is through ChronoSync's system menu in the menu bar. Here are the steps needed to accomplish this:

  1. Click on the ChronoSync system menu in your menu bar.
  2. Under the "Scheduled Syncs" submenu, highlight the document you wish to sync.
  3. Hold down the OPTION key and click on the highlighted document.
  4. The sync will now run in the background.
