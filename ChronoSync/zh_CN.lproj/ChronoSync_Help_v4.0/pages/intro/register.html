Introduction

Register ChronoSync

  1. Open ChronoSync.
  2. From the ChronoSync menu, choose "License..."
  3. The License Panel will appear. Notice that the status is "Demo Mode".
  1. Click on the "Register" button.
  2. The Registration Panel will appear.
  3. Choose whether you want to register for all users of the computer or just the current user.
  4. Supply a "User Name", "E-mail" and the "License Key" that was mailed to you upon completion of your purchase.
  1. If the license key is valid, the "Register" button will highlight.
  2. Click "Register" and a "successfully registered" window will appear.
  1. Click OK and the License Panel will reappear with an "Enabled" status.
  1. Once you click OK, you are registered and can begin using ChronoSync fully.
