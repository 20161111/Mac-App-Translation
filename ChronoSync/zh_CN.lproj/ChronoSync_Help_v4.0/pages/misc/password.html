Password Protected Documents

You may designate a password which must be entered before a specific Synchronizer or Container document can be opened. Access restriction is a lightweight form of security. It does not encrypt the contents of the document, it just makes it a bit inconvenient for prying eyes.

To Password Protect a Document:

  1. Choose "Access Restriction..." under the "File" menu.
  2. A sheet will drop down for you to enable access restriction and to specify a password.
  3. Once all information is entered, choose "OK".

The next time you attempt to open the protected Synchronizer or Container document, a window will come up for you to supply a password. If you've supplied the correct password, the document will open.

Note: Access restrictions will not interfere with a Synchronizer document's ability to be scheduled and/or run from the scheduler. It simply limits the ability of users to view/edit Synchronizer documents without knowing the password.
