Customizing The Toolbar

All the tools necessary to manage the synchronization of targets are available on the Synchronizer document, Container document and log viewer toolbars. Choose “Customize Toolbar...” from the "View" menu to select the items that you would like to have on the toolbar. To place a new item, drag the desired tool to the toolbar. Once placed, the tools can be rearranged by dragging them to their desired location. Note that the available toolbar options are slightly different depending on which OS you are running.
