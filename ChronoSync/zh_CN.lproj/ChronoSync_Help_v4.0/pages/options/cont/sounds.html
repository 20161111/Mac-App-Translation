Sounds for Containers

In the "Sounds" section, you can specify unique sounds to be played on error or successful completions and when your attention is needed. Assigning a different sound for each option can help you identify the document's state without actually having to access the document. ChronoSync can run in the background and you can easily monitor it. Container document sounds apply to the container as a whole and do not override individual Synchronizer documents' sounds.

Sounds options explained:

Play sound on success - Will play the chosen sound when a synchronization successfully completes.

Play sound on failure - Will play the chosen sound when a synchronization fails to complete without errors.

Play attention sound - Will play the chosen sound when a synchronization requires the user's attention.

Note: Choosing the "Default" sound results in the sound specified in Preferences being used. In general, you would want to use the Default settings and specify sounds in Preferences, but you may have a "special" Synchronizer document that you want to uniquely identify completion and/or errors by using a different sound.
