Deleting Rules

While in the "Simple" rule mode, there is no way to delete a rule. You can, however, disable a rule so that it will not be considered for synchronization. To do this, simply uncheck the rule that you no longer wish to have.

In the "Intermediate" or "Advanced" rule-base mode, rules in either the inclusion or exclusion list may be easily deleted by following the steps below.

To delete a rule from an inclusion or exclusion list:

  1. Select the rule that you wish to delete by highlighting it in the list.
  2. Click on the icon.
  3. When the warning message appears, verify deletion by choosing "Remove".
  4. The rule will no longer appear in the list.
