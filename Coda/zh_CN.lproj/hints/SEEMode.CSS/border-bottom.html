border-bottom

properties of the bottom edge of the element's border, such as width, style, and color

Description

properties of the bottom edge of the element's border, such as width, style, and color

This is a shortcut property that sets the border-bottom-width, border-bottom-style, and border-bottom-color properties in one statement. It's not necessary to provide all three values, but they must appear in that order.
