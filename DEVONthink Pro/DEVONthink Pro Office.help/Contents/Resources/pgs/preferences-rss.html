    RSS

DEVONthink Pro Office comes with built-in RSS capabilities that enable you to add RSS feeds to your database and have DEVONthink Pro Office update them automatically.

RSS feeds are represented in DEVONthink Pro Office like smart groups, which update themselves with content from the Internet. You create these feeds with Data > New > Feed and use them like any other group.

Use the RSS preferences to define how often DEVONthink Pro Office checks for new articles, whether to skip duplicates when updating feeds, when to remove older articles, and which CSS style sheet to use for displaying the articles. Unread or locked articles are never automatically removed. Check Also unread articles to also remove unread articles when cleaning up, and check Convert categories to tags to tag imported articles automatically if the feed contains categories.

DEVONthink Pro Office comes with multiple styles for displaying feed articles. You can use your own RSS stylesheets by placing them into "~/Library/Application Support/DEVONthink Pro 2/StyleSheets".
