    Update

Change the settings in this tab to define when you want DEVONthink Pro Office to check for new versions: never, on startup, daily, weekly, or monthly. DEVONthink Pro Office only performs checks for updates when the computer is connected to the Internet.

Click Check Now to immediately check for a new version.

Note: Disable the check for new versions when your network connection is unstable and you experience long delays when opening DEVONthink Pro Office. The Update pane is not available if you have purchased DEVONthink Pro Office from the Mac App Store.
