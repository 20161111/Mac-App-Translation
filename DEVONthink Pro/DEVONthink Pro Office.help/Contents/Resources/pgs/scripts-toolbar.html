    Toolbar scripts

You can also write scripts that you add to any toolbar of a DEVONthink Pro Office window. This way you can extend DEVONthink Pro Office's functionality nearly endlessly.

To add a toolbar script place the script into the folder "~/Library/Application Support/DEVONthink Pro 2/Scripts/Toolbar" (where "~" represents your home directory). To navigate to this folder visit the Go menu in the Finder with the ⌥Option key held which makes the "Library" menu item visible. Then right- or ⌃Control-click the toolbar of a DEVONthink Pro Office window and choose Customize Toolbar. Drag your script into the toolbar.
