    Archive your messages

DEVONthink Pro Office is the repository for emails you want to save.

Its hierarchical groups structure and automatic classification function are perfectly suited to drop new messages into the database, sort them, and help you find them again. DEVONthink Pro Office comes with advanced email archiving functions that transfer selected messages or mailboxes from Apple Mail, Microsoft Entourage, and any email applications using standard Unix mailboxes, e.g., Thunderbird or GNUmail to a DEVONthink Pro Office database. The original structure of the message, as well as all attachments, are preserved. You even canreply to messages directly from within DEVONthink Pro Office.

Possible uses:

  • Archive important correspondence
  • Archive invoices, offers, purchases, or software licenses
  • Store interesting newsbits from newsletters
  • Keep project-related emails and other documents together
