    Recovering a lost password

If you have protected a database and you have forgotten your password, do the following to remove the password protection:

  • Close DEVONthink Pro Office
  • Locate your protected database in the Finder
  • Right/Control-click it and choose Show Package Contents
  • Move the file "Settings.plist" to the Trash

A future version of DEVONthink Pro Office will feature industry-strength encryption.
