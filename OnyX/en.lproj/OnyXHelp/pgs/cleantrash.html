Trash

Some files or folders are not easily deleted, particularly when they are locked or when you don't have write permission.
To remove Trash contents for all accounts on all connected disks, regardless of privileges:

■

Use the Automation pane.

or

■

Use the Trash pane of the Cleaning toolbar item.

The Secure Delete option overwrites every byte of deleted contents three times (with 0xff, 0x00, and 0xff again). Therefore, this operation takes a very long time. Wait for the task to complete.

Warning: This option is not recommended with a solid-state drive or a Fusion Drive because that reduces the drive's lifespan.
