Inactive elements in the Apple menu ()

If the Restart, Shut Down, and Log Out menu items in the Apple menu don't work, the kiosk setting is probably turned on. To resolve this issue:

Open OnyX, and enter your password.

Click the Parameters toolbar button, and select the Login tab.

Deselect the "Turn off the Restart, Shut Down, and Log Out menu items in the Apple menu" box.

This issue sometimes occurs after updating the system.
