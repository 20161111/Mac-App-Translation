Problem with the Open With shortcut menu of the Finder

If the Open With shortcut menu of the Finder displays duplicate applications or applications that are not installed anymore, OnyX can help you correct the problem. You should let OnyX rebuild the LaunchServices database.

Open OnyX.

Open the Automation pane, or select the Rebuilding tab of the Maintenance toolbar item.

Select the LaunchServices checkbox, and deselect the other checkboxes.

Click the Execute button.
