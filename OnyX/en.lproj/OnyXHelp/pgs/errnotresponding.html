OnyX is not responding

During the execution of most of these commands, it is not possible to perform other commands simultaneously because of a risk of conflict. For this reason, you will see the message "Application not responding" in the Dock menu or "OnyX (not responding)" if you try to force OnyX to quit.

Some operations take a long time (such as verifying the structure of the system files, repairing permissions, rebuilding the locate database, and so on).

Wait for the tasks to finish!
