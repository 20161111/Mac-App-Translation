Impossible to check the structure of the disk

If the startup disk cannot be verified, the journaling is probably turned off.

The journaling maintains a permanent record of changes made to files on the disk. If your computer stops because of a power failure or some other issue, OS X uses the journal to restore the hard disk to the last acceptable state before it was stopped.

Turning on journaling is highly recommended.

To turn on journaling:

Open OnyX, and enter your password.

Click the Info toolbar button, and select the Disk tab.

Click the Turn On button.
