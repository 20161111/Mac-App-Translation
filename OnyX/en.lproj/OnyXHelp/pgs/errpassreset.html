If you forget your administrator password

If you forget your administrator password, you can reset it using Apple's built-in Recovery HD menu.

Quit OnyX.

Restart the computer while holding down Command-R until the OS X Utilities pane appears.

In the menu bar, choose Utilities > Terminal.

In the Terminal window, enter the following: resetpassword

Press the Return key.

Follow the onscreen instructions to reset your password.

Be sure to change the password for your login keychain to match your new account password so your keychain is unlocked when you log in.
