The colored pinwheel appears

The colored pinwheel is a wait cursor, and it doesn't necessarily mean that a "crash" has occurred or that your system has stopped responding. It can appear during operations that take a long time to complete.

For example, the colored pinwheel can appear in the following operations:

■

Verifying structure of the system files

■

Repairing permissions

■

Creating and rebuilding locate database
