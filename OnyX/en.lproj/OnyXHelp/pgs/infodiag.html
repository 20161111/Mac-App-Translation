Diagnostic

You can use the Diagnostic pane of the Info toolbar item to execute a full diagnostic examination.

This option collects a huge amount of data then compresses this information and generates a file (sysdiagnose_xxx.tar.gz) on the desktop.

This diagnostic tool, primarily intended for technicians and advanced users, can also be used by Apple for troubleshooting needs, to investigate issues, and to help Apple improve its products.
