Log

The Log stores the date, hour, and description of every operation executed by OnyX. You can click the Log toolbar item or go to Window > Open Log Window to view the Log file. From within the Log window, you can reveal the Log file in the Finder or delete the Log.

By default, the OnyX Log is deleted when quitting the application, but you can retain it by deselecting the option default in the Actions pane of OnyX preferences.
