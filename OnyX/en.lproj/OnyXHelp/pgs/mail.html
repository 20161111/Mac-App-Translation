Mail's mailboxes

You can use the Rebuilding pane of the Maintenance toolbar item or the Automation pane to rebuild Mail's mailboxes index.
This option can prevent and correct issues concerning incomplete, truncated, or missing messages.

From the Maintenance > Rebuilding pane, you can also delete the existing index. At the next launch of Mail, the application will ask you to re-import the emails, and the index will be automatically rebuilt.
If Mail not responding, search is not fucntioning correctly, or the application won't launch, this option can correct these issues.
