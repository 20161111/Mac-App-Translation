Preferences — General

You can use the General pane of OnyX preferences to modify the appearance of OnyX.

■

You can use a local menu to choose the pane to automatically open when you open OnyX.

■

You can turn on the "Drawer" graphic effect when opening OnyX panes.

■

You can use the Appearance pop-up menu to choose an Aqua or Graphite user interface.

Note: Appearance-changes will be enabled the next time OnyX is opened.

■

You can use the Icon pop-up menu to change the application icon.

Note: You must restart the computer to display the new icon in the Finder and in the Dock.

■

You can reduce the opacity of the main window by moving the Opacity slider; this takes effect immediately.

Click the Restore Defaults button to cancel your modifications and restore OnyX to its default setting.
