Splitting a Clip
To split your clip into two smaller clips, position the scrubber over the moment where you want the split to occur, then press the T key or select Edit > Split. This splits the selected clip at the scrubber into two separate clips. By default, the newly-created clip to the left is selected after the split.
Note: Press Shift-T to split the clip and have the clip on the right selected.
