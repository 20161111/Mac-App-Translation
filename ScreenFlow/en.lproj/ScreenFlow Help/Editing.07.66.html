Configuring Annotations Properties
Annotations are used for displaying simple shapes in your project. Annotations are clips you can add to your timeline, and behave like image clips. You can apply several video properties (scaling, position, x/y/z rotation, and opacity) and video actions to annotations clips.
To display the Annotations Properties pane, click the Annotations Properties icon in the Properties pane, as shown in Figure 51 below.
