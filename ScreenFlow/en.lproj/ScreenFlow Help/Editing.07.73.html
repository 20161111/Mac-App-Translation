Removing a Filter
To remove a filter that has been added to a clip, follow these steps:
1.
Select the clip that you want to remove the filter from.
2.
Select the Video or Audio properties pane, as appropriate.
3.
Click on the Video | Audio Filters disclosure triangle to display the filters Figure 55.
Figure 55. Deleting a Filter from a Clip
4.
Scroll through the filters until you find the one you want to delete.
5.
Click the X icon to delete it from the clip.
Note: You can disable a filter temporarily, by unchecking its name.
