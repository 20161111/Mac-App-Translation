Removing ScreenFlow from Your Computer
To uninstall ScreenFlow, open the Applications folder and drag the ScreenFlow application to the trash. (The ScreenFlow license is stored separately, so you do not need to re-activate your license if you reinstall ScreenFlow.)
The files that you remove depend on the versions of ScreenFlow you have had on your computer.
