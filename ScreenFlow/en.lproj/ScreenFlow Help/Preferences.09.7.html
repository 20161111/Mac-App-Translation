When ScreenFlow is licensed, the Licenses pane enables you to deactivate serial numbers, as depicted in Figure 82.

Deactivate Serial Number
Click to deactivate your ScreenFlow license. This allows you to use your license on a different computer. Please allow up to 15 minutes to process the deactivation before re-activating this serial number.
