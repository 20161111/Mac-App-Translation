Placing a Clip Spatially
To place a clip anywhere on the canvas, click and drag it to the location you want, and then release the mouse. When dragging a clip on the canvas, as you near the center vertically or horizontally, yellow guidelines appear to help you locate the clip along those lines, if you want.
The location of a clip is also specified as part of its properties. To adjust it there, select the clip and click its Video Properties tab. The location is identified in the Position field, as X and Y values (in pixels).
