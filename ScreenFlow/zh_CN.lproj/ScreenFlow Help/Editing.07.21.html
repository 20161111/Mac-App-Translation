Adding and Deleting Tracks
You never need to add a track explicitly; ScreenFlow adds a track every time you drag a clip down into the timeline below the existing tracks.
To delete a track, use one of these methods:
•
Right-click in the track controller to display the context menu and select Delete
•
Click in the track controller and drag it out of the window and drop it when the cloud displays.
