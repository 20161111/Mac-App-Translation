Adding Media to the Timeline
To add media to your timeline once it’s in the media library, either drag it to the canvas or the timeline which will place it wherever the scrubber is on the timeline. You can also double click on the media object to add to the timeline at the scrubber location. You can select one or more clips using lasso selection—click and drag across some portion of each clip to select it.
For complete details on these and other ways to add media to your project, see Adding Media to Your Project.
