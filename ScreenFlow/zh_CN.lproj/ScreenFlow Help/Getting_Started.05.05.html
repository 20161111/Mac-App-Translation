Tour 2– Editing Your Screencast
The purpose of this tour is to familiarize you with the basic editing features of ScreenFlow. This tour describes some of the most common and basic editing techniques.
For a comprehensive description of editing features, see Editing ScreenFlow Projects.
When you finish your recording, ScreenFlow creates a new document and recorded media is placed at the beginning of the timeline. The media elements on the timeline reference your project’s stored media. Changes made on the timeline or on the canvas do not alter your original recording.
