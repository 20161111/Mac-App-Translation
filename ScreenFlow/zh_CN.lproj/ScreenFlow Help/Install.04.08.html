Deactivating Your ScreenFlow License
If you want to use your ScreenFlow license on another computer, you must deactivate it first. Then, you can activate the license in ScreenFlow running on another computer
Note: This topic does not apply to users who purchased from the Mac App Store. The App Store has its own installation and licensing procedure.
Your user must be an administrator. If your user is not an administrative account, youâ€™ll need to create one (or log on with one) in order to continue.
To deactivate your ScreenFlow license, follow these steps:
1.
Start ScreenFlow.
2.
Select ScreenFlow > Preferences and click the Licenses tab, as shown in Figure 5.
Figure 5. Preferences Licenses Pane
3.
Click the Lock icon at the bottom of the pane, and enter the password to your administrative account to allow ScreenFlow to make changes to your license.
4.
Click Deactivate to deactivate the license on this computer. Now, you can use the same license in ScreenFlow running on another computer. Please allow 15 minutes before trying to reactivate your license.
