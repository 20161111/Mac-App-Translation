Setting Preferences
You use ScreenFlow Preferences to set your options and customize ScreenFlow to suit your specific needs. To display Preferences, select ScreenFlow > Preferences or press Command-comma.
Note: If you purchased ScreenFlow through the Mac App Store, the License tab does not display. The Mac App Store has its own licensing and updating processes.
