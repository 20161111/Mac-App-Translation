Spaces

Script Debugger can show space characters in text. They are shown as small dots. To show space characters:

  • You must first be showing invisible characters. Then:

    • Choose View > Show Spaces.

    • Or, choose Show Spaces from the contextual menu.

    If the menu item is checked (and invisibles are showing), spaces are showing.
