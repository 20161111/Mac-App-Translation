Line Numbers

Script Debugger can display your script with line numbers in the gutter. To toggle the visibility of line numbers:

  • Choose View > Show Line Numbers.

  • Or, choose Show Line Numbers from the contextual menu.

If the menu item is checked, line numbers are showing.

The navigation bar also shows line numbers, and you can jump to a line by its number.
