Arrows and lines

Use the Arrow tool to create vector-based arrows or lines. Choose from a variety of arrows in the styles gallery or create your own. Tools and effects are only available for image captures.

Add an arrow or line:

  1. Select the Arrow tool on the Tools tab.
  2. Click to select a style from the styles gallery or adjust the properties to create your own arrow or line style.
  3. Click and drag on the canvas to add the arrow.
  4. To add a curve to the arrow, click and drag the yellow center point.
