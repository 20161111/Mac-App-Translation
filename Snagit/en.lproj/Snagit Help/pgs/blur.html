Maintain privacy with the Blur tool

Use the Blur tool to mask or hide sensitive content within an image capture such as usernames, email addresses, passwords, contact information, etc. Tools and effects are only available for image captures.

Blur an area:

  1. Select the Blur tool on the Tools tab.
  2. Click and drag to blur an area on the canvas.
  3. Drag the slider in the properties pane to adjust the amount of the blur.
  4. To move the blur, click within the blur and drag it to a new position on the canvas.
  5. To resize the blurred area, click to select the blurred area on the canvas. Drag a handle in or out.
