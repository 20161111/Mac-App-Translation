Import media from a mobile device

TechSmith Fuse is a mobile app that allows you to send images and videos from your mobile device directly to Snagit. You can capture, collaborate, and access your content - wherever you are.

Fuse is available from the App Store, Google Play, or the Windows Store and supported on mobile devices running the following operating systems:

  • iOS version 7 and later (phones and tablets)
  • Android version 4.0 and later
  • Windows 8.1 or Windows RT

Import images or videos from a mobile device

  1. Install TechSmith Fuse from Google Play, App Store, or Windows Store onto your mobile device.
  2. In Snagit, select File > Connect Mobile Device. Or, select Connect Mobile Device from the Snagit menu bar icon.
  3. Connect your computer and mobile device to the same network. See Troubleshooting TechSmith Fuse.
  4. Open TechSmith Fuse on your mobile device. Select an image or video to send.

  5. Tap Connect to Snagit.
  6. The Scanner appears. Point your camera at the QR code in the Connect to Mobile Device dialog. See Tips for scanning the QR code.

The image or video is sent to the Open Captures Tray in Snagit Editor.

Note: You can only preview and share the videos within Snagit Editor. To edit the video, import the video capture into Camtasia.

Tips for scanning the QR code

  • Make sure the device is on and connected to the same network as the computer running Snagit.
  • Aim the camera on the device at the QR code on the computer screen.
  • Position the camera so that the QR code is centered within the corners of the Scanner screen in TechSmith Fuse on your mobile device or tablet.

About connecting TechSmith Fuse and Snagit

  • Opening the Connect Mobile Device dialog turns on the Mobile Sharing option.
  • You must scan the QR code in Snagit to establish a connection between the mobile device and Snagit for sending the mobile files.
  • Your mobile device and the computer running Snagit must be connected to the same network.
  • You must have Snagit open on your computer.
