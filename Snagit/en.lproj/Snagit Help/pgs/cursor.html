Capture and edit the cursor

Capture the current position of the cursor in an image capture. In Snagit Editor, you can move, resize, or delete the cursor in image captures.

Capture the cursor:

  1. On the Capture window, click the Additional options and help button.
  2. Select the Capture Cursor option.

Or, select Snagit > Preferences > Capture tab > Capture cursor.

TIP: Use a capture keyboard shortcut to maintain the position of the cursor.

Move or resize the cursor in an image capture:

  • To move the cursor, click and drag the cursor on the canvas.
  • To resize the cursor, click to select the cursor on the canvas and drag a handle.

To add a different cursor to your image capture, use the Stamp tool.
