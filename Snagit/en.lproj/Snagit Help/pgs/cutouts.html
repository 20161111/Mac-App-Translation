Cut out

Delete a vertical or horizontal section of the capture and join the two pieces together with or without an accompanying edge effect.

Cutting out a section makes the graphic smaller and more compact while maintaining the integrity of the whole image.

Cut out unwanted areas:

  1. Click the Cut Out button.
  2. The cut out styles appear above the button. Select the desired style.
  3. A blue line appears on the canvas. Click and drag the area you want to cut out on the canvas.
