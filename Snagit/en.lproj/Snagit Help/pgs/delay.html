Set a capture delay

The Delay capture option adds extra time before starting a capture to allow you to set up items on the screen such as opening a menu.

Set a capture delay:

  1. On the Capture window, select the Additional options and help button.
  2. Enable the Delay capture option and select the amount to delay.

    You can also set a delay by selecting Snagit > Preferences > Capture tab > Delay capture by.
  3. Click the Capture button or press Shift-Control-C. A countdown appears in the bottom right corner of the screen.
