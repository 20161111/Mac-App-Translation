Draw tools: Fill, Pen, Highlighter and Eraser

Tools and effects are only available for image captures. Share your video capture to Camtasia to enhance your video with annotations and effects.

Flood-fill an area with color or change the background color:

  1. Select the Fill tool on the Tools tab.
  2. Click to select a color from the styles gallery or adjust the properties to create your own fill style.
  3. Click on the canvas to apply the fill.

Draw with the freehand pen:

  1. Select the Pen tool on the Tools tab.
  2. Click to select a style from the styles gallery or adjust the properties to create your own pen style.
  3. Click and drag on the canvas to draw with the pen tool.

Highlight an area:

  1. Select the Highlighter tool on the Tools tab.
  2. Click to select a style from the styles gallery or adjust the properties to create your own highlighter style.
  3. Click and drag on the canvas to add a highlight.

Erase a portion of a capture:

  1. Select the Eraser tool on the Tools tab.
  2. Click to select a style from the styles gallery or adjust the properties to create your own eraser style.
  3. Click and drag on the canvas to erase portions of the capture.
