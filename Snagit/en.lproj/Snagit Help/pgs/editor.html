Editing in Snagit

Snagit Editor opens:

  • After a capture, if the Preview in Editor option is enabled. See Capture preferences.
  • When you select the Snagit menu bar icon > Open Snagit Editor.
  • When you click the Open Snagit Editor button in the Capture window.

  • Canvas: Working area to preview, edit, and assemble captures. See Canvas.
  • Open Captures Tray: Shows the recent captures and opened images. The capture selected in the tray is shown on the canvas. See Open Captures Tray.
  • Tools: Tools to enhance or annotate your image capture. See Tools.
  • Effects: Add effects to your image capture such as an edge effect, border, shadow, perspective, reflection, and color filter. See Effects.
  • Styles Gallery: Provides preset and customized styles. See About the Styles Gallery.
  • Properties: Customize the settings for the drawing, annotations, and effects. On the Tools tab, the available properties appear below the styles gallery. On the Effects tab, click to view the effect's properties. See Adjust the Tools or Effects properties.
