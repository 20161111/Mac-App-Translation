Export a file to OneDrive for Business to access your files at any time across computers or devices.

Export to My Places

  1. In Snagit Editor, select File > Export to My Places.
  2. Select a location from My Places.

    If you do not have any locations in My Places or to export to a new location, see Add or remove a Place.
  3. Enter a filename.
  4. Click the Select button.

The capture uploads to the selected location.
