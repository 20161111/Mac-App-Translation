Share to Facebook

The Facebook output uploads video or image captures onto Facebook.

Share to Facebook

  1. Click the Facebook output button or select Share > Facebook.
  2. If you have previously shared to Facebook, continue to step 3. If you have not previously shared to Facebook:
    • Click Add Account.
    • Enter your email and password.
    • Click the Next button.
    • Click Sign In.
  3. Select the privacy level, where to post the capture (your wall or an album), and enter text for the caption.
  4. Click Post.

Snagit posts the capture and caption to your Facebook account.
