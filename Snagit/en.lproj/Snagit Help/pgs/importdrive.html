Import images or videos from Google Drive

Use Google Drive to store images or videos so they are accessible on any device at anytime.

Import images or videos from Google Drive

  1. In Snagit Editor, select File >Â Import from Google Drive.
  2. If you have not previously logged into your Google account, enter your email and password and click Sign in.
  3. Click Accept in the Connect with Google Drive dialog.
  4. The Import from Google Drive dialog appears. Click to select the image or video to import into Snagit. Command-click to import multiple images or videos.
  5. Click the Import button.

Snagit adds the images or videos into the tray in Snagit Editor.

Note: To complete additional video editing, import the videos into Camtasia for Mac. You can only cut, preview, and share video captures in Snagit Editor.
