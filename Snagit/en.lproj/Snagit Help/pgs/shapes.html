Shapes

Use the Shape tool to create vector-based shapes. Choose from a variety of shapes in the styles gallery or create your own. Tools and effects are only available for image captures.

Add shapes:

  1. Select the Shape tool on the Tools tab.
  2. Click to select a shape from the styles gallery or adjust the properties to create your own shape style.
  3. Click and drag on the canvas to add the shape. Press the Shift key while dragging to draw a perfect circle or square.
  4. To edit the shape, click to select the shape on the canvas.
    • Move shape: Click within the shape and drag it to a new position on the canvas.
    • Resize shape: Drag a handle in or out.
    • Flip shape: Select Arrange > Flip Horizontal or Flip Vertical
    • Rotate shape: Drag the green handle around the shape.
