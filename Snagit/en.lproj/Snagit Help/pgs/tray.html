About the Open Captures Tray

The tray shows your recent captures or opened images. The capture selected in the tray is shown on the canvas.

Open Captures Tray

  1. The most recent capture appears here in the tray.
  2. Click the button to remove the capture from the tray. Removing a capture from the tray does not delete the capture. The capture will be located in the saved location or in the Unsaved Captures folder.
  3. Selected Capture: The capture highlighted in blue in the tray is the capture shown on the canvas. Click to select the capture to display on the canvas.
  4. If an image in the tray has been saved, the file format appears in the lower right corner of the thumbnail in the tray. To save an image, select File > Save or Save As.
  5. Click the left or right arrows to control which capture appears on the canvas or to navigate between captures in the tray.
  6. Click to hide or show the tray.
  7. Unsaved Captures Folder: Opens any unsaved captures. Snagit automatically stores unsaved captures as a Snagit project file (.snagproj) in Documents > Snagit > Autosaved Captures folder.
  8. Unsaved Changes: Select File > Save or Command-S to save the current changes.
  9. Video captures display the video duration on the bottom right of the thumbnail. To preview the video, click to select the video in the tray. The video and playback controls appear on the canvas.

Other tips:

  • Control-click on a capture in the tray to access additional context menu options.
  • You can combine captures in the tray. Click to select a capture and press Command-C to copy the capture. Click to select a capture to paste it into and press Command-V.
