Video selection guides

When selecting the area to record for a video capture, the video guides show standard dimensions for common sharing destinations.

If you plan to share your video to a location that requires specific video dimensions, it is recommended to record at these dimensions or at the same aspect ratio as the recommended dimensions.

The following are the standard video dimensions:

  • (320 x 240), 4:3 aspect ratio
  • 480p (640 x 480), 4:3 aspect ratio
  • 720p (1280 x 720), 16:9 aspect ratio
  • 1080p (1920 x 1080), 16:9 aspect ratio
  1. When selecting an area to record, press G to turn the guides on or off.
    • Hold CTRL to lock the selection to a 4:3 aspect ratio.
    • Hold SHIFT to lock the selection to a 16:9 aspect ratio.
  2. Click and drag to select the recording area. As you drag the cursor near one of the standard dimensions, the guide dimly appears
  3. Continue to drag the cursor until the label highlights and the border matches the guide.
