Capture an open or hidden window

Window Captures helps you to quickly locate a window to capture. Use Window Capture when you have several windows opened or the window to capture is hidden behind other open windows.

Capture an open or hidden window

  1. Press the Window Capture keyboard shortcut: Shift-Control-W.
  2. Move your cursor over the window to capture.
  3. The window highlights. Click to capture the highlighted window.

Note: Window capture does not capture the cursor.
