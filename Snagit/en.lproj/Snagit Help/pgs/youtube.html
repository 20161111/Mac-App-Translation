Share to YouTube

Upload a video to YouTube for video hosting. YouTube provides a URL for quick sharing or an embed code to embedding the video onto a website or blog.

YouTube resizes the video to fit within their standard dimensions. For best results, record at YouTube's recommended dimensions.

Share to YouTube

  1. Click the YouTube output button or select Share > YouTube.
  2. The YouTube Login dialog appears if you have not saved your YouTube account settings in the Snagit Preferences. See Customize the YouTube account settings below. Enter your login information and click the Log In button.

Snagit uploads the capture onto YouTube. When the upload is complete, a message appears informing you that the capture link or embed code is on the clipboard, ready for pasting and sharing into an email, instant message, or another location. The video may take some time to process on YouTube. The video will appear in My Uploaded Videos.

Customize the YouTube account settings

You can customize, save, and set up multiple YouTube accounts from the Output Preferences dialog.

  1. Select the More Outputs button and click the Output Preferences button.
  2. Click the YouTube option. The YouTube output settings appear below.
  3. Click Log in to set up an account username and password. Select Save Password in Keychain to remember this account. Click Log In.
  4. From the Clipboard contents dropdown, select one of the following:
    • URL: Provides the YouTube video URL after uploading to allow you to quickly paste the URL for sharing.
    • HTML Embed Code: Provides the embed code after uploading to embed the capture on a website or blog.
  5. Select the privacy level for the video.
  6. Close the Preferences dialog.

Snagit remembers these YouTube account settings. To share a capture with these settings, click the YouTube output button above the tray.
