Fix scrolling capture

In Snagit, you can capture the entire scrollling area in a window, document, or web browser, beyond the area visible on the screen. You can install additional options to improve Snagit's scrolling capture capabilities from this dialog.

Install options to capture scrolling areas:

  1. Select Snagit > Preferences > Capture tab.
  2. Click the Enable button.
  3. Click the Open System Preferences button.
  4. Select Privacy > Accessibility.
  5. Click the Lock icon in bottom left corner of the dialog. Enter your password.
  6. Under "Allow the apps below to control your computer," enable Snagit and SnagitHelper (if listed).
