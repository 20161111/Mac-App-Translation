Share to Camtasia for Mac

The Camtasia output imports the selected capture into the Media Bin and onto the timeline in a Camtasia project. Use Camtasia for Mac to:

  • Import still images to use as source files in your Camtasia video.
  • Enhance your video capture with callouts, text, titles, captions, effects, and much more.

Learn more about enhancing and editing your video capture in Camtasia here.

Share to Camtasia

Click the Camtasia output button or select Share > Camtasia.

You must have Camtasia for Mac version 1.2 or later installed for the Output button to appear.

Snagit imports the video or image capture into the Media Bin and timeline in a Camtasia project.
