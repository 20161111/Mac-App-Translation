Cut, copy, or paste a capture

You can cut, copy, and paste the following in image captures:

  • Objects such as the cursor or annotations
  • A selection
  • The entire canvas or image capture

Cut or copy entire canvas:

  1. Click to select a capture in the tray.
  2. Select Edit > Copy. Or, use the keyboard shortcut: Cut (Command-X) or Copy (Command-C).

    Tip: To maintain the placement of all the objects and captures on the canvas, flatten the objects to make the objects a permanent part of the image. To flatten an object, control-click on an object and select Flatten or press Command-Shift-T. After you flatten an object, you cannot select, move, or modify the object.

Copy capture to Clipboard:

  1. On the Capture window, click the Addional options and help button.
  2. Enable the Copy to Clipboard option or select Snagit > Preferences > Capture tab > Always copy captures to clipboard.
  3. Click the Capture button.
  4. Press Command-V to paste the capture from the Clipboard into a document or application.

Cut or copy a selection:

  1. On the Tools tab, click the Selection tool.
  2. Click and drag to make a selection on the canvas.
  3. Select Edit > Cut or Edit > Copy. Or, use the keyboard shortcut: Cut (Command-X) or Copy (Command-C).

Paste a selection or capture:

Select Edit > Paste or press Command-V to paste a selection or capture.
