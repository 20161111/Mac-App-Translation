Share to Dropbox

Upload the image or video capture to Dropbox for hosting. Snagit copies a link to the capture onto the Clipboard.

Share images or videos to Dropbox

  1. In Snagit Editor, select an image or video capture in the tray.
  2. Click the Dropbox output button or select Share > Dropbox.
  3. If you have not previously logged into your Dropbox account, enter your email and password and click Sign in.
  4. Click Allow.
  5. The Share to Dropbox dialog appears. Enter a file name, select the folder to upload the capture to, and click Upload.

Snagit uploads the image or video into the selected Dropbox folder and copies the URL onto the Clipboard. Videos may take some time to process in Dropbox.

Customize the Dropbox account settings

You can customize, save, and set up multiple Dropbox accounts from the Output Preferences dialog.

  1. Select the More Outputs button and click the Output Preferences button.
  2. Click the Dropbox option. The Dropbox output settings appear below.
  3. Change the desired settings.
  4. Close the Output preferences.

Dropbox account settings:

  • Sign Out: Click this button to sign out of the selected Dropbox account.
  • Folder: Select the default Dropbox folder to upload images and videos to.
  • Ask for filename and location each time: Enable this option to prompt to enter a file name and select the location with each upload to Dropbox.
