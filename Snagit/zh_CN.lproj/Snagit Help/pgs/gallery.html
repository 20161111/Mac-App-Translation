About the styles gallery

The styles gallery provides preset styles for the Tools and Effects. You can add your own custom styles to the gallery. Tools and effects are only available for image captures.

Apply a style from the gallery (Tools):

  1. Click the Tools tab.
  2. Select the desired tool. The styles available appear below the tools.
  3. Click to select a style from the styles gallery.
  4. Depending on the tool selected, click or click and drag the canvas to add the style.

Apply a style from the gallery (Effects):

  1. Click the Effects tab.
  2. Click to apply a style from the gallery. Effects apply to the entire image.

Remove Effect(s):

  • Click the effect(s) in the Effects tab to disable.
  • Or, click the No Effects style in the gallery.

Add a custom style to the gallery:

  1. Select the desired tool or effect(s) and edit the properties.
  2. As you edit the properties, the customized style appears in the styles gallery. If you hover over the custom style, you can view the specific properties.
  3. Click the Add Style button to permanently add the style to the gallery.

Delete a style from the gallery:

Click the Delete button on the style.

You cannot restore customized styles. To restore the default styles, see Advanced preferences.
