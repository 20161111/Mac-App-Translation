Share to Google Drive

Upload the image or video capture to Google Drive for hosting. Snagit copies a link to the capture onto the Clipboard.

Share images or videos to Google Drive

  1. In Snagit Editor, select an image or video capture in the tray.
  2. Click the Google Drive output button or select Share > Google Drive.
  3. If you have not previously logged into your Google account, enter your email and password and click Sign in.
  4. Click Accept in the Connect with Google Drive dialog.
  5. The Share to Google Drive dialog appears. Enter a file name, select the folder to upload the capture to, and click Upload.

Snagit uploads the image or video into the selected Google Drive folder and copies the URL onto the Clipboard. Videos may take some time to process in Google Drive.

Customize the Google Drive account settings

You can customize, save, and set up multiple Google Drive accounts from the Output Preferences dialog.

  1. Select the More Outputs button and click the Output Preferences button.
  2. Click the Google Drive option. The Google Drive output settings appear below.
  3. Change the desired settings.
  4. Close the Output preferences.

Google Drive account settings:

  • Add Account: Select this option to set up and remember a Google account.
  • Account dropdown: Select the default account to upload to from the Google Drive Output button.
  • Sign Out: Click this button to sign out of the selected Google account.
  • Folder: Select the default Google Drive folder to upload images and videos to.
  • Ask for filename and location each time: Enable this option to prompt to enter a file name and select the location with each upload to Google Drive.
