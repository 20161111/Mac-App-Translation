Take multiple captures

After taking a capture, Snagit automatically opens the capture in the editor for previewing and editing. You can disable the Preview in Editor option to take multiple captures before previewing.

Take multiple captures:

  1. In the Capture window, click the More Options button and disable the Preview in Editor option. Or, select Snagit > Preferences > Capture tab and disable the Preview in Editor option.
  2. Click the Capture button or use a capture keyboard shortcut.

Snagit temporarily saves the capture(s) in the Autosaved Captures folder. The captures appear in the tray when you open the editor.
