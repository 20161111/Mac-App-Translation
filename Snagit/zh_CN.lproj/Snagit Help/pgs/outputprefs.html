Manage the Output preferences

The Output buttons in Snagit Editor allow you to quickly share an image or video capture to popular destinations. To learn more about each output, see Snagit outputs.

You can customize the following items from the Outputs tab in Snagit's preferences:

  • Save or change your account settings.
  • Set up multiple accounts for the same output, such as a business account and a personal account.
  • Add or delete an output.
  • Control the arrangement of the Output buttons in Snagit Editor.

Open the Output preferences

  • Select Snagit > Preferences > Outputs tab.
  • Or, click More Outputs > Output Preferences button.

Add a new Output button

  1. Select Snagit > Preferences > Output tab.
  2. Click the Add Output button and select an output from the list.
  3. Enter a button name. The button name appears when you move the mouse over the Output button in Snagit Editor.
  4. Set the Output settings below. For more information on each output's settings, see:
    • Email a capture
    • Share to Screencast.com
    • Share to Google Drive
    • Share to Camtasia
    • Share to TechSmith Relay
    • Share to YouTube
    • Share to Dropbox
    • Share to Twitter
    • Share to Facebook
    • Share to Evernote
    • Share by FTP
    • Copy All
    • Share to OneDrive for Business
  5. Close the Preferences dialog.

Delete an Output button

  1. Select Snagit > Preferences > Output tab.
  2. Click and drag the output out of the dark gray area.

To restore a recently deleted output button, click the Add Output button and select Recently Deleted.

Rearrange the Output buttons

Select Snagit > Preferences > Outputs tab.

Tip: Move the outputs you use most frequently to the left of the dotted line.
