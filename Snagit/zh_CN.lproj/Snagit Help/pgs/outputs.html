Snagit Outputs

With Snagit's Outputs, you can:

  • Quickly share an image or video capture to popular destinations with the Output buttons in Snagit Editor.
  • Save your account settings.
  • Set up multiple accounts for the same output, such as a business account and a personal account.
  • Control the arrangement of the Output buttons in Snagit Editor.

See Manage the Output preferences.
