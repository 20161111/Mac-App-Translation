Share a capture

The Output buttons in Snagit Editor allow you to quickly share an image or video capture to popular destinations. The Output buttons are located above the tray in Snagit Editor.

To learn more about each output, see Snagit Output overview.

Share a capture using the Outputs

  1. Click to select the capture to share in the tray.
  2. Click an Output button or select an option from the Share menu. Snagit disables any Outputs that do not support the file format of the selected capture.
  3. The first time you share to an output, you will need enter your account information. For more information on saving and managing your Output account settings, see Output preferences.

Snagit uploads the capture to the selected Output.
