Preview a capture

After taking a capture, Snagit automatically opens the capture in Snagit Editor.

Preview an image capture:

Image captures display at their actual size on the canvas. To zoom in or out on the canvas:

  • Select View > Zoom In or Zoom Out. Or, press Command-Plus to zoom in by 20% or Command Minus to zoom out by 20%.
  • Click the Show Zoom Controls button. Drag the Zoom slider or enter a zoom percentage into the field.

When the canvas is zoomed in to a high percentage the entire capture may not be visible. To view the hidden portion, hold the spacebar while dragging on the canvas.

  • To view the capture at its original size, select View > Actual Size or press Command-0.
  • To fit the capture to the canvas window, select View > Zoom to Fit or press Command-Shift-0.

Preview a video capture:

The video capture preview is scaled to fit on the canvas. If you hover the cursor over the video on the canvas, the video playback controls appear.

  1. Elapsed Time: Jumps to the beginning of the video capture.
  2. Start handle: Drag the handle to select the starting point for the video cut.
  3. Mute/Volume Slider: Click the Mute button to mute audio playback in the video. Drag the volume slider to adjust the playback volume.
  4. Playhead: Controls which frame in the video is displayed. Drag the playhead along the timeline to jump to a specific point in the video.
  5. End Handle: Drag the handle to select the end point for the video cut.
  6. Previous Frame: Click to move to the previous frame in the video. Hold down the button to rewind the video.
  7. Play/Pause: Play or pause the video playback.
  8. Next Frame: Click to move to the next frame in the video. Hold down the button to fast forward the video.
  9. Time: Displays the total duration of the video capture.
  10. Capture Frame: Saves the current video frame as a still image file. The image opens in the tray.
