Make a selection on the canvas

Use the Selection tool to select a portion of a capture to copy, cut, paste, or to move a selected area to a new location within the image.

Tools and effects are only available for image captures.

Make a selection:

  1. On the Tools tab, click the Selection tool.
  2. Select a shape.
  3. Click and drag on the canvas.

Select objects on the canvas:

  • To select an object, Shift-click an object to include in the selection.
  • To toggle an object in or out of a group of selected objects, Command-click the object.
