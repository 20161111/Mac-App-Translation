Upload a capture to OneDrive for Business to access it at any time across computers or devices.

Share to My Places

  1. Click to select a capture in the tray.
  2. Click the OneDrive for Business output button or select Share > OneDrive for Business.


    If you have not previously shared to OneDrive for Business, see Add or remove a place.
  3. Enter a title.
  4. Click Browse to select a location.
  5. Click Upload.

Snagit uploads the capture to the selected Place and copies the URL onto the clipboard.
