Stamps

Use the Stamp tool to add graphics such as accents, keyboard keys, interface, cursors, and numbers to your captures. Tools and effects are only available for image captures.

To download more stamps, click the Get More Stamps button in the Stamp properties.

Add a stamp:

  1. Select the Stamp tool on the Tools tab.
  2. Double-click to view the stamps within a folder.
  3. Click to select a stamp from the folder.
  4. To add the stamp, click on the desired position on the canvas.
  5. To move the stamp, drag the stamp to a new position on the canvas.
  6. To resize the stamp, click to select the stamp and drag a white handle in or out.
  7. To rotate the stamp, click to select the stamp and drag the green handle around the stamp.
