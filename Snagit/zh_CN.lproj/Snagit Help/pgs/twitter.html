Share to Twitter

The Twitter output posts a tweet on Twitter that includes a link to the image or video capture.

Share to Twitter

  1. Click the Twitter output button or select Share > Twitter.
  2. If you have previously shared to Twitter, continue to step 3. If you have not previously shared to Twitter:
    • Click Add Account.
    • Enter your email and password.
    • Click the Next button.
    • Click Sign In.
  3. Enter your tweet text in the dialog. As you type, the number of characters remaining displays in the bottom right corner of the dialog.
  4. Click Send.

Snagit posts the tweet to your Twitter account.
