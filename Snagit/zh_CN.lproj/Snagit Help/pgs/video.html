Record a video

Video capture records the action on the screen and audio (from a microphone or system audio) as a MPEG-4 video file. Use the Snagit video to:

  • Walk someone through a process or issue.
  • Record a demonstration or how-to video.
  • Give audio and visual feedback.
  • Record a small portion of a video to playback in a presentation.

1. Select the video recording area

Snagit offers the flexibility to select the desired recording area including the entire screen, a specific region or window, or at standard video dimensions.

Learn how to adjust the recording area to get exactly what you need.

See Select the video recording area.

2. Set up audio recording

Narrate your video by recording microphone audio or system audio (the sound from your computer speakers).

See Set up audio recording.

3. Start and stop the video capture

Learn more about the hotkeys and controls to start, pause, and stop video capture.

See Start and stop a video capture.
