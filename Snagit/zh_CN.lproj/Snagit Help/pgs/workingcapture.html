Capture window

Use the Capture window to start an image capture, video capture, or to change the capture settings and preferences.

View the capture options

Move your cursor over the blue bar on the right side of the screen to expand the Capture window.

The Capture window can be docked anywhere along the left or ride sides of the screen. Click and drag the Capture window to change its docked locaton.

Taking a capture

To start an image or video capture, click the red Capture button. See Capture an Image or Record a Video.

To start another type of capture, click the More Capture Types button and click a capture type from the list.

Additional capture options

  1. Open Snagit Editor: Open Snagit Editor to preview, edit, and share captures.
  2. Additional options and help: Click to change the capture keyboard shortcuts, set additional capture options, or get help.
  3. Keyboard Shortcuts: The capture keyboard shortcuts allow you to start a capture without interacting with the Capture window. To edit the Global Capture, Image Capture, or Video Capture keyboard shortcuts:
    • Change the keyboard shortcut: Click within the keyboard shortcut field. Press the desired key combination on the keyboard. The new keyboard shortcut appears in the field.
    • Delete the keyboard shortcut: Click in the keyboard shortcut field.
  4. Capture Cursor: Capture the cursor as an editable object in image captures. Snagit automatically captures the cursor in video captures.
  5. Copy to Clipboard: Automatically copy the capture to the Clipboard. Press Command-V to paste the capture from the Clipboard into another application or document.
  6. Preview in Editor: Open the capture in Snagit Editor to preview, edit, or share the capture.
  7. Delay capture: Set up a delay before capture to allow for additional time to set up the screen or to open a menu. See Set a capture delay.
  8. More Preferences: Open the Capture tab in the Preferences dialog for more capture options.
