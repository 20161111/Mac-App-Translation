## ...and More

Also, look for the following features in the various menus – they should be pretty self-explanatory:

- Version History (“File › Browse All Versions…”)
- Spelling and Grammar, Substitutions, Dictation etc. (“Edit” menu)
- Text Zoom (“View › Zoom”)
- Typewriter Scrolling, Line Highlight (“View” menu)
- Full Screen Mode (“View › Enter Full Screen”)
